About Me

I’m an undergraduate at UWE (University of the West of England) Bristol studying a Games Technology degree with the ambition of becoming a fully-fledged games developer myself. Currently I’m working my way through my second year.

I wouldn’t be studying this degree without a passion for all things gaming, unique and immersive games are what appeal to me and what drives me forwards is the hope of someday helping create a game that makes people just stop and say “Wow”.
